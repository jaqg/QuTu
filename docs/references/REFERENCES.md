# References

## NH3 Umbrella Inversion

- Swalen, J. D., & Ibers, J. A. (1962). Potential function for the inversion of ammonia.
  *J. Chem. Phys.*, **36**(7), 1914–1918.
  https://doi.org/10.1063/1.1701290

- Townes, C. H., & Schawlow, A. L. (1955). *Microwave Spectroscopy*. McGraw-Hill.

- Harris, D. O., Engerholm, G. G., & Gwinn, W. D. (1965). Calculation of matrix elements
  for one-dimensional quantum-mechanical problems and the application to anharmonic
  oscillators. *J. Chem. Phys.*, **43**(5), 1515–1517.
  https://doi.org/10.1063/1.1696963

- Levine, I. N. (2014). *Quantum Chemistry* (7th ed.). Pearson.
